Crispy French fries

Crispy french fries with a delicious cheese sauce.

This recipe was from nino's kitchen

Ingredients

  • 800g potato
  • 1Tsp salt
  • 20g of corn starch
  • 20g Unsalted butter
  • 5g flour
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • 1/4 Chilli powderTsp Salt
  • 100 of milk
  • Black pepper
  • 70g of cheddar cheese
  • 10g sugar

Steps

  1. cut 2 -3 potatoes
  2. soak in bowl of water for 25 min
  3. strain and wash potatoes a few more times
  4. turn on a pot and put some water with a pinch of salt, then add your fries
  5. boil for 5 min
  6. strain the potatoes after boiling
  7. put potatoes in bowl and put 20g of cornstarch and shake
  8. turn on a pan and put a generous amount of oil
  9. then put your fries on medium high heat for 5-8 min
  10. let them cool
